Product Review

The Smart Coast is an IoT device designed to promote healthy hydration habits and help the user stay hydrated throughout the day to support energy and productivity. The coast is equipped with a weight sensor that detects changes in the water cup's weight, tracking the amount of water consumed, and a humidity&temperature sensor to decide the ideal interval of water consumption. After each drinking action, the device uses Wi-Fi to transmit the time and amount of water consumed to a connected mobile app. This data is synchronized with the device in real time via Wi-Fi. The device also includes a buzzer to remind users to drink water if the cup has been idle for too long.
